• SEDPI Socially Responsible Investments

  • What is SEDPI and SRI ?

    SEDPI Development Finance, Inc. (SDFI) was incorporated in the Philippines on March 10, 2008 under republic Act No. 8556 or the Financing Company Act with company registration number CS200803725.

    Socially responsible investments (SRI) is also known as impact investing where investments are made to companies, organizations and funds with the intention to generate a measurable, beneficial social or environmental impact alongside a financial return.

    SDFI is engaged in the sale of socially responsible investments in the form of preferred shares and resale of loan portfolio. Preferred share stock certificate or certificate of assignment of receivables are given to social investors as proof of their investment.

    The proceeds are used to invest in a well-selected portfolio of debt and equity securities mostly to microfinance institutions and social enterprises operating in the Philippines; as well al small scale real estate projects. The Company is designed to accommodate small social investors seeking capital appreciation and positive social impact.

  • Frequently Asked Questions

    1. Can we add investments anytime? Yes, with a minimum of Php 1,000.00
    2. Can we withdraw our investments? How? Yes, you can. Just visit SEDPI Social Investor Online , select I am a SEDPI social investor and use the code emailed to you.
    3. How long is the withdrawal process? Usually two weeks.
    4. Can I have a beneficiary? You are required to elect at least one beneficiary.
    5. Is the dividend rate the same every year? No, it depends on the performance of the organization and is set every January.
    6. Is there a passbook? No. You will receive a stock certificate representing preferred shares.
